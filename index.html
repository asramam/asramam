About The School


SMSN is the perfect place for your child to prosper both academically as well as for becoming a responsible citizen.The values of Sreenarayana Guru is being passed onto our students.We guide our children in the right path and help them achieve their purpose in life.We ensure you that the students of SMSN school Vaikom has all the opporunities to study and excel.The shool was built in 1949 the memory of the great Sree Narayana Guru's lead in the Vaikom Satyagraha Temple Entry Proclamation 1924–25.Many great leaders including Mahatma Gandhi, TK Madhavan etc. has visited the school at the event of Vaikom Sathyagraha
